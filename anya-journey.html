The Path of Awakening

Anya's Journey.

From the bustling noise of the physical world to the profound silence of true energetic alignment. Explore the roots of a spiritual visionary.

The Call to Spirituality

Anya's path didn't begin in a monastery orashram. It began as a profound internal calling amidst an ordinary existence, a whisper that eventually became a roar. Seeking deeper meaning, she embarked on a transformational odyssey across the sacred sights of Bali, the ashrams of India, and the ancient ruins of Greece.

Immersed in indigenous traditions and modern somatic practices, she learned to bridge the gap between ancient wisdom and contemporary existence, establishing a deeply grounded approach to spiritual awakening.

Divination & Tarot

More than just reading cards, Anya approaches Tarot as a profound psychological mirror. It accelerates the breaking down of subconscious barriers, serving as an intuitive blueprint for the soul's current terrain.

Her readings weave together archetypal psychology and energetic channelling, providing profound clarity and actionable wisdom for those navigating life's most complex transitions.

Energetic Healing

Anya is a master practitioner of somatic breathwork and vibrational healing. She believes that the body holds the score, keeping the memories and traumas that the mind attempts to forget.

By guiding breath patterns and utilizing frequency alchemy, Anya creates a safe, expansive container that allows stagnant energy to move, releasing deep-seated blocks and opening the heart to a frequency of profound love and acceptance.

Creating Retreats

Today, Anya pours her decade of intensive learning and practice into curating the flagship experiences for SSC Events. Her vision is to create sanctuaries where others can shed the layers of societal conditioning and re-bloom into their truest, most authentic selves.
