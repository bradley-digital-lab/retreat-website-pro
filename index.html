Our Ethos

Spaces for awakening,
connection and true transformation.

At SSC EVENTS, we believe transformation begins with the space to pause and reconnect. Our gatherings are intentional sanctuaries created for you to explore your inner world, expand awareness, and remember who you truly are.

Through conscious community and shared experience, we hold environments where connection deepens and meaningful change unfolds—guiding you back to your inner power and the unique path your soul is calling you toward.

Founder & Facilitator

Anya Lily.

Founder of Spiritual Soul Centre · Space Holder · Retreat Facilitator

Anya Lily is the creative force behind SSC Events. Her journey began early, with a natural sensitivity to the unseen world that evolved into a lifelong quest for consciousness. By seventeen, she was reading tarot professionally—a prelude to her deep work in intuitive mediumship, hypnotherapy, and multidimensional soul work.

Rooted in lived experience through pregnancy, birth, and healing, Anya creates sanctuaries where people can reconnect with their true power.
